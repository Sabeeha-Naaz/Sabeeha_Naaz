A Little Bit About Your Trainer

Sabeeha Naaz is a distinguished mentor in public speaking and creative writing, known for her exceptional ability to teach students across the globe. With over 1,000 students positively impacted by her guidance, she has established herself as a leading figure in the field. Her extensive educational background includes a Master's in English Literature and a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), showcasing her deep understanding of the subject matter and her commitment to the craft of teaching.

Sabeeha's qualifications are further solidified by her success in clearing both central and state-level teacher eligibility tests, reflecting her competence and dedication to upholding high standards in education. With 2 years of hands-on teaching experience at the secondary level in a CBSE board school, she has honed her skills in shaping young minds, preparing them for academic success and beyond.

In addition to her secondary-level experience, Sabeeha has also taught graduation-level students, where she has played a pivotal role in refining their communication and writing abilities. Her global teaching influence and strong academic credentials make her an inspiring and effective mentor, committed to empowering students with the skills they need to excel in their educational and professional journeys.
